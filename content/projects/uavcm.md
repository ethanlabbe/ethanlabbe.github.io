UAVCM is a Python program developed under the supervision of Dr. James Myatt for the Dean's Research Award. This project involves the creation of a 6-degree of freedom control model for an underwater vehicle autonomous vehicle. Applying learned knowledge of control theory, Python programming, and underwater vehicle dynamics I created graphical simulations by solving a system of differential equations to predict the vehicle's depth under varying conditions, based off of a predicted mathmatical model for the vehicle.
